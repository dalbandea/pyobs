Eigenvalue problem
==================

.. math::
   V^{-1} A V = \Lambda \quad \Lambda = \mathrm{diag}(\lambda_0, \lambda_1, \dots \lambda_n)

.. math::
   \delta \Lambda  = V^{-1} [\delta A] V

.. math::
   \delta V_{ij} = \sum_n \frac{V_{nl} V_{jk} }{\lambda_n - \lambda_i} V_{in} \delta A_{kl}
